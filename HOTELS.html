UZBEKISTAN HOTEL

Hotel details This upscale hotel in a striking, high-rise building with Asian influences is set in the city's business district and is a 3-minute walk from the nearest metro station. It's 6 km from Chorsu Bazaar in the city's old town. The subdued, modest rooms offer free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and minifridges. Suites have an old world vibe and add separate living rooms. Upgraded suites add separate private offices. Room service is available 24 hours. Breakfast is free and served in 1 of the hotel's 2 restaurants. There's also a 24-hour cafe, a gym and a beauty salon. Massage services and an area shuttle (fees may apply) are available.
